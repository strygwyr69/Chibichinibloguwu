Self Care & Self Improvement

How to love the most important person in your life: YOURSELF

Written by Chini

Nov. 10, 2020

• READ

Books are the food for your mind. Read as much positive, empowering books as you can to make you mind stronger.

• MUSIC

Listen to calming, soothing music to calm your mind. The sound of raindrops falling or just calm music without any lyrics also helps.

• MEDITATE

Meditation is the best way to free up your mind, to get your mind out of all the thoughts you don't want. If you cant focus, try lighting a small candle, keep it in front of you, sit and try to focus only on the flame and nothing else till the candle lights out. Keep increasing the size of candle time to time till you think you can focus without it.

• TAKE A GOOD LONG SHOWER

Take a hot relaxing two hour shower to soothe your body and to feel like the fresh cutest human you are.

• TREAT YOURSELF

Treat yourself with presents, food, get out of the house, get all the things you want.

• ON A DRIVE/ WALK

Go on a long drive or a walk by yourself to clear your mind and freshen up.

• EXERCISE

Have a good work out session to keep your mind and body fit. Go gym, if you have a busy routine, go for the 7- minute exercises available online whenever you have some free time once a day.

• COOK AND EAT

Cook new and different meals every week and try new varieties, cook yourself and your loved ones some bomb meals.

• LEARN AND CREATE

Create, draw, write new things make some sketches, write about the events in your day in detail, write your goals and aims, make a bullet journal and make a goal to do everything written in it- alone or with a loved one. Find new hobbies, practice them, learn new languages or new art forms or anything that catches your attention or that you want to do.

• CLEAN

Clean your room, rearrange your house create it as you like do some DIY decorations for your house.

• MAKE A SLEEPING ROUTINE

Make a proper fixed sleeping routine for every day. Make sure you get 7 to 8 hours of sleep daily. Take power naps to boost up your energy in between house work or studying at home but make sure the power nap doesn't exceed 25 minutes or it'll make you lazy instead of boosting you up.
